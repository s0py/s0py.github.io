Work

Below is a list of notable creative works I've been a part of.

Community Lead Deliberation Event

This was an event me and 4 other students lead as part of our Rhetoric and Civic Life course while at Penn State. We reached out to community leaders in healthcare and criminal justice as well as the general public to congregate and deliberate solutions to the ongoing Opiod Epidemic. I was in charge in making the informational packet summarizing the talking points prepared by my groupmates.

Link to the PDF here

Mock TED Talk

As part of a Speech Class, I gave a mock TED Talk on a topic of my choosing. It is available here.


The Great Debate | Penn State Econ Association

This is an annual event in which 6 students are split into right-wing and left-wing teams and make presentations on a chosen topic. My team was designated the "Socialism" side in the "Capitalism v. Socialism" debate in 2019. There was sadly no recording but I and my team won by a landslide in the post-debate audience poll. There was an amazing turnout of around 500 Penn State economics students (and my parents).
